# Privacy Policy for PG Management System

**Last Updated:** May 21, 2026

**Effective Date:** May 21, 2026

## Introduction

PG Management System ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("PG Management System" or the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Register for an account
- Use our App features
- Contact us for support

The personal information we collect may include:
- **Name**: To identify users and tenants
- **Email Address**: For account login and communication
- **Phone Number**: For contact and SMS notifications
- **Property/Company Information**: For PG business management

### Tenant Information

As a PG owner/manager using our App, you may input:
- Tenant names and contact information
- Tenant documents (ID proof, address proof)
- Rent and payment details
- Room/bed allocation data
- Complaint and maintenance records

**Note:** You are responsible for obtaining necessary consent from tenants before inputting their information into the App.

### Device Information

We automatically collect certain information when you access the App:
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information
- App usage statistics

### Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for:
- Rent payment reminders
- Complaint updates
- System announcements
- Important alerts

You can disable push notifications through your device settings.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and Maintain the App**: Enable core functionality like tenant management, rent tracking, and notifications
2. **Improve User Experience**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
3. **Send Notifications**: Deliver important updates and reminders
4. **Customer Support**: Respond to your inquiries and troubleshoot issues
5. **Security**: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
6. **Legal Compliance**: Meet regulatory requirements

## Data Storage and Security

### Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:
- Cloud servers with industry-standard encryption
- Secure database connections
- Regular backups and redundancy

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:
- Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
- Secure authentication protocols
- Access controls and monitoring
- Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
